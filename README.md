**👋 Hi, I'm Barry Quinn (@quinfer)**

I'm Professor of Finance and Financial Technology at Ulster University Business School. My background combines nine years as a currency trader and liquidity manager in London with fifteen years of academic work as a Chartered Statistician and applied econometrician.

My research sits at the intersection of statistical learning, econometric inference, and financial institutions and markets, with a particular focus on algorithmic accountability and the governance of algorithmic systems in finance.

**🔬 Research Themes**

- Trustworthy quantitative methods for financial markets
- Statistical learning and econometric inference under model uncertainty
- Algorithmic accountability and the governance of AI in finance

**🤝 Collaboration**

I welcome enquiries from researchers, PhD candidates, and industry partners working on reproducible empirical methods, open-source statistical tooling for finance, and the responsible deployment of algorithmic systems in regulated markets.
